# Background Music

## 목적

정보 과부하에서 생산성 향상으로 이어지는 감정 변화를 음악으로 표현

## Suno Prompt

Modern corporate technology commercial soundtrack.

Starts with subtle tension and digital atmosphere representing information overload and mental clutter.

Gradually transitions into an uplifting and inspiring productivity-focused theme.

Clean electronic textures, soft piano accents, modern synth elements, motivational energy, premium SaaS brand commercial style.

Professional, cinematic, emotional progression from chaos to clarity.

No vocals.

Duration: 35 seconds.
